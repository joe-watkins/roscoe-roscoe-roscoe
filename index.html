Roscoe, Roscoe, Roscoe

A ‘kitty cat’ who needs no introduction...

Every now and then you run into a critter that stands out from the rest of the pack. Roscoe is an American Shorthair tabby who loves to eat, sleep, play, and chat. If he's not patrolling the backyard you can find him curled up inside, or yammering at his human's for more food. Seems like everyone who runs into him loves him and can't resist his charm and dapper looks.
